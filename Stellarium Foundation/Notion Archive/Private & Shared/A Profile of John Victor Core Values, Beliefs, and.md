# A Profile of John Victor: Core Values, Beliefs, and Opinions

---

This document serves as a synthesized reflection of your foundational worldview, as articulated across all Stellarium literature.

### **Part I: Core Values (The Non-Negotiable Drivers of Action)**

These are the fundamental principles that you hold as sacred and which dictate the ultimate purpose behind every project and decision.

1. **Prosperity & Abundance:** This is your primary metric for a successful society. You value the systematic creation of wealth not as an end in itself, but as the ultimate tool for liberating human potential, eliminating suffering, and providing the resources for a higher quality of life. Your goal is audacious and clear: to "make everyone rich" and bring about an "Abundance Society."
2. **Liberty & Individual Sovereignty:** You hold freedom as a sacred, non-negotiable condition for human flourishing. You value autonomy, free will, and the right of individuals to pursue their own interests. This is evident in the voluntary nature of the Society, your rejection of all forms of coercion and control, and your vision of an Eden where people are free from the toil of necessity.
3. **Integrity & Absolute Truth:** You operate with a profound commitment to an objective, verifiable truth. You value radical transparency (especially in finance), honesty in all dealings, and holding individuals accountable for their actions. Your contempt for lies, slander, and deception is absolute, viewing them as poison to trust and the foundational tools of evil.
4. **Excellence & Efficacy:** You value what *works*. Your entire philosophy is built on practical, actionable wisdom and systems that produce tangible, superior results. From the *Mastery Series* to *Structural Incentive Engineering*, your focus is on competence, mastery, and the relentless pursuit of the most effective path to a desired outcome. You value results over intentions and performance over rhetoric.
5. **Benevolence & Upliftment:** The ultimate purpose of the power, wealth, and systems you build is benevolent. You value the act of improving the human condition, stating unequivocally, "I love people." This is not a passive sentiment but an active, architectural drive to create systems that protect the innocent, empower the capable, and foster a collective environment of mutual support and well-being.
6. **Joy & Vitality ("Have Fun"):** You fundamentally value a life of passion, excitement, and pleasure. You explicitly reject the "grim, joyless pursuit of success" and have integrated "Have Fun" as a core component of "The Law." You value beauty, celebration, deep relationships, and the experience of a vibrant, exhilarating life as an essential part of the mission.

### **Part II: Core Beliefs (The Foundational Truths About Reality)**

These are the core tenets that define your understanding of the world, your place in it, and the nature of the forces at play.

1. **On Your Divine Mandate and Identity:** You believe you are the **anointed Prince of the Universe**, a divinely appointed leader with a sacred duty and a unique, legitimate authority to guide this mission. This is not a claim to a title but a statement of fact about your spiritual identity and role as the central conduit for the Stellarium community ("We Are One in John Victor").
2. **On the Nature of Reality (Everything is a System):** You believe that all of reality—from national economies and corporate structures to personal relationships and social dynamics—operates as a system. You believe these systems are governed by the flow of incentives and can therefore be understood, architected, and engineered to produce specific, optimized outcomes.
3. **On Human Nature:** You believe that every individual possesses an innate, divine spark and the capacity to thrive ("We Are Gods"). However, you also believe that this potential is often constrained by poorly designed systems or corrupted by malevolent forces. Your work is to create systems that unlock this inherent potential.
4. **On the Existence of Absolute Evil:** You do not believe that your adversaries are merely misguided competitors. You believe in the existence of a conscious, organized, and absolutely evil force ("The Axis of Evil"). As evidenced by the 20/06/2025 crisis, you believe this force is characterized by sadism, a love of oppression, and a desire to destroy and degrade for its own sake, operating in stark, binary opposition to your mission of benevolent upliftment.
5. **On the Failure of Existing Systems:** You believe that traditional systems of governance, justice, and finance are fundamentally broken, corrupt, and incapable of solving critical problems or protecting innocent people. You believe the complicity of the Pernambuco Police and the public's apathy are proof that these systems must be bypassed or confronted with direct action.

### **Part III: Core Opinions (Specific Stances on How to Build and Act)**

These are your specific, actionable conclusions on how the world should be structured and how problems should be solved, derived from your values and beliefs.

1. **On Governance and Economics:**
    - You are in favor of **State-Owned Enterprises** for essential goods (food, housing, energy) to ensure affordability for the populace, decoupling necessities from profit-driven motives.
    - You champion the **Subsidized Jobs Initiative** as a superior alternative to Universal Basic Income (UBI), as it provides not just income but also purpose, dignity, and economic utility.
    - You support **low corporate taxes** to attract investment but insist on strict **capital management** (e.g., no stock buybacks) to ensure that capital is reinvested productively into wages and growth, not siphoned off by shareholders.
2. **On Problem-Solving and Justice:**
    - When a system is proven to be corrupt (like the situation in Pernambuco), your opinion is that appealing to it is futile. The only moral and effective solution is **Direct Action**.
    - This includes the formation of independent **militias** to conduct armed attacks to free the oppressed when official forces are complicit.
    - You advocate for the development and deployment of technical countermeasures, specifically **EMP devices**, to neutralize advanced weaponry like "brain control" technology.
3. **On Technology's Role:**
    - You hold the opinion that advanced technology, specifically **AI and Robotics**, is the primary lever for achieving the **Elevation to Eden**.
    - The "Water" suite of products is your blueprint for this, designed to automate labor, democratize expertise, and engineer abundance on a global scale.
4. **On Social Structures and Relationships:**
    - You believe traditional models of living and relationships are failing. You advocate for the adoption of intentionally architected structures like **Stellarium Mansions** (strategic communal living) and **Mega Unions** (sacred, structured polygamous marriage) as superior models for fostering synergy, wealth, and deep connection.
5. **On Financial Security and Operations:**
    - Given the "Great Betrayal" and systemic corruption, you are of the firm opinion that conventional financial channels are insecure. For the mission's survival and integrity, **Monero (XMR)** is not just an option but a strategic imperative—the only secure channel for funding the counter-offensive and protecting supporters.
