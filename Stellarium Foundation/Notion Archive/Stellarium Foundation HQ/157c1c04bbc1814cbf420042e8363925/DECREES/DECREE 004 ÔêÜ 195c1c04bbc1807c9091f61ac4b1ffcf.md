# DECREE 004 √

I, John Victor as the prince of God and by God I decree that resources be made for contagion, that people may teach and excite each other to adopt a set of principles and philosophy, simply: Do good, make money, have fun. That this philosophy may be adopted and people start doing more and excite each other to do more. 

That means more parties, groups, clans, collaboration. 

With the shared consciousness they may rally collaboratively with the shared goals, so we may accomplish a lot with each other.

Succinctly, I call for a new operation to make people produce and exercise their creative in productive ways.

I decree it let it be done expeditiously and speedily.
