I, John Victor as the prince of God and by God I decree that investigation be done on the false witnesses that have risen up in the past against the prince, make diligent search on whoever accused the prince of any serious crime with malicious intent of harming the innocent on official channels such as the police, and ask the where, when, who exactly was involved, and exactly what the allegation is, and investigate through the archives, if the person is found to be a false witness, the procedure is: If they have lied for gain or to make money they have to return 50% of everything they have made financially by being a false witness, if they they refuse to return the money, execute the false witness with the firing of bullets.

All of the money recovered shall be given to the Stellarium Foundation’s [Bank Account](../Stellarium%20Hub/Bank%20Account%201acc1c04bbc1816a9476dd65fd2e6a77.md).

I decree it let it be done expeditiously and speedily.
