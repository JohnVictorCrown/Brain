# How To Monetize Your Work On The Internet

Let's say you have a series of social media on the internet, TikTok, YouTube, Instagram, etc… You need to monetize your work, if you're an influencer, model, artist or internet personality you need to find ways to monetize it. 

Here are the ways:

- Affiliate Marketing: Earn commission on the products you reference to sell, or website signups for gambling, financial services, or any website or service that has affiliate programs.
- Donations and Crowdfunding: Earn money by receiving donations on social media, an opportunity neglected on some platforms.
- Subscription on Platforms: Earn money by offering subscriptions to your online persona and content, on platforms or Patreon.
- Sponsorships and Brand Advertising: Reach out to brands you like or be reached out from brands that like your branding, and provide advertising as posts, usage or endorsement for money. With sponsorships you can also make a contract with a brand and become an official brand ambassador or endorser and make money from it.
- Platform Ads: Work with platforms to maximize your revenue from ads from the platform itself.
- Launching Your Own Product: Leverage your branding and social media to launch any product or enterprise and potencialize it. It can be anything from water to fashion lines to merchandising to useful products.
- Launching Digital Products: Courses, mentorship, e-books and art. Make money by sharing more of your content in a streamlined manner.
- Create a Blog, Podcast, Website Or Newsletter: Monetize these channels through ads run on the platform.
- Launch Paid Events: Launch paid events like webinars and conferences and create revenue
