# How to Solve Poverty

We have come with this question posed by one of our clients: I will give you $Y Billion to solve world hunger if you can solve world hunger with $Y Billion.

Excited for the challenge we geared up our multidisciplinary research team to come with a project, it has been an amazing quest with a lot of discoveries and innovative advances we accomplished in many other initiatives unthought of initially.

Solving world hunger involves setting up a couple of institutions to advise on the administration and implementation of resources, instead of the usual approach of buying the goods e.g. food and distributing it, which, even though it serves it's purpose in contingency situations it is not an efficient nor sustainable approach to solve the situation.

To solve world hunger, we developed a couple of initiatives to directly impact food production: The Enterprise Farming Pledge, The Advertising Funded Product Production, and the Global Agriculture Task Force, which is not posted on this In.

The Enterprise Farming Pledge increases the supply of food and the proceeds from it can be used to fund other agricultural projects further, the Enterprise Farming Pledge can be implemented with a rather small team of activists and employees.

The Advertising Funded Product Production also works to decrease food prices and fund other agricultural Projects, it can be implemented with a rather small amount of personnel and it produces revenue that pays the initial investment.

The Global Agricultural Task Force works with a team of researchers to implement efficient and sustainable farming practices where the expertise and technological "know how" is scarce, lecturing local communities, setting up wells, farms and working on desertification projects with voluntary workers are common practices. The projects have expected proceeds so the investments can be further funded.

In our quest we realized a lot of the world hunger stems from general poverty and badly designed economic environments that doesn't foster prosperity so we initiatives such as the Fostering the Efficiency and Affordability of Essential Goods, and advised many of our clients on *low corporate tax.
