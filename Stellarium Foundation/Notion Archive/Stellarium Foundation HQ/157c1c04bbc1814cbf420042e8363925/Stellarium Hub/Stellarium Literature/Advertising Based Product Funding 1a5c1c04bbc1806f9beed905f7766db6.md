# Advertising Based Product Funding

There already are initiatives such as the @freewater.io company which leverage clever business models to market a bottle of water which costs nothing to the clientele and is totally funded by advertising placed on the bottle.

The genius of this business cannot be understated, and inspired by it we devised to our partners another innovative way towards the economy of the future, where the basic goods are cultivated to have a high availability to the whole society, curbing poverty and increasing discretionary spending on the broad economy.

In synthesis the steps are:

- Pick products which are staple goods i.e. widely needed, frequently used such as food products, salt, cereal, rice, sugar, wheat flour etc.... Criteria is how wide is the demand and need and how much people tend to use the product.
- Contact advertisers and supporters, present the project and set the ads pricing to fund the costs of the product.
- Obtain product from the supplier and package it.
- The packaging is important, metal casing and elaborate designs with durable materials is a must because the advertising and the whole project ideas are encapsulated in it.
- The product is whole for free or at symbolic price since some retailers are more willing to accept a product with a price tag.

That's how you can create products and sell them at symbolic pricing to supply the broad economy with basic goods, which effectively increases the populace's purchasing breadth in other sectors. This is another active collaborative philanthropy initiative.

So in an active collaborative philanthropy how would the whole production work?

To market a basic food product, let's say rice:

- At first you could actually just use the advertising based funding to just supply the rice and package it in an interesting, charming and useful package at much lower than market cost.
- To actually farm the rice we would foster an Enterprise Farming Pledge to actually have our own supply of rice.

So it goes: Use the Enterprise Farming Pledge to farm the rice, and use the advertising funded products to market the rice at decreasing costs.

We were hired by amazing partners to devise ways to "make everyone rich" i.e. solve world hunger, curb extreme poverty and such goals. That's what the Stellarium Foundation is about and I'm happy we are accomplishing it.
