# The Stellarium Foundation: Q&A

**Section I: The Foundation & Its Mission**

**Q1: What is the primary goal of the Stellarium Foundation?**
Our primary and immediate goal is **to create collective value for people on a global scale.** We achieve this by architecting systems, launching innovative products, and providing world-class advisory services that generate real wealth, solve critical problems, and enhance human potential. Our mission is practical and results-oriented, as detailed in our [**Wealth Activism and Human Endeavours**](Wealth%20Activism%20and%20Human%20Endeavours%20Real%20Justice%20%201a5c1c04bbc18080a97fc05be1dc8b4c.md) initiative.

**Q2: What are the core principles that guide the foundation?**
We are guided by a clear set of foundational tenets that form the basis of all our work. These are our guiding lights for all decision-making:

- ∆ Each Individual possesses the innate ability to thrive on their own interests and pursuits.
- ∆ Wealth is above all the biggest and most important metric, therefore generate wealth.
- ∆ The duty of every one is to be a peacemaker; peace and wealth walk in one accord.
- ∆ War is anti-wealth; conflict is anti-peace.
- ∆ Improving and serving people creates wealth.
- ∆ Human creativity and ingenuity drive innovation.
- ∆ **The Law is: Do Good, Make Money, Have Fun.**
You can review the full context of these in [**The Principles**](../The%20Principles%201b0c1c04bbc1804b945edcaf2757d0d7.md) document.

**Q3: Can you expand on "The Law: Do Good, Make Money, Have Fun"?**
This is our core operational ethos, detailed extensively in our foundational [**Little Book**](Little%20Book%2019fc1c04bbc18002af8cd007c09be756.md). It breaks down as follows:

- **Do Good:** This is our commitment to benevolence, fairness, and positive impact. It means loving goodness, standing up for people, being a hero, and creating tangible solutions like the [**Enterprise Housing Pledge**](Enterprise%20Housing%20Pledge%201a4c1c04bbc180518daaf78ef83966cc.md).
- **Make Money:** This is our primary metric for success. Wealth is created through brilliant innovation, superior service, and collaborative effort. We believe in generating immense value and ensuring prosperity is shared by all.
- **Have Fun:** We believe that the process of creation should be joyful, engaging, and fulfilling. We foster a vibrant global community where passion, pleasure, happiness, and self-fulfillment thrive.

**Q4: Is the Stellarium Foundation a non-profit or for-profit entity?**
It strategically functions as both. The Stellarium Foundation is the guiding non-profit institution that undertakes missions with broad societal impact. The [**"Water" suite of products**](../Products%20And%20Projects%2019cc1c04bbc1802dbca5cca213940cb7.md), however, are designed as for-profit ventures. The revenue from these ventures is intended to be reinvested to fuel the foundation's broader, benevolent mission, creating a powerful, self-sustaining ecosystem.
