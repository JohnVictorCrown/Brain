### **Document 4: Operational Plan**

**Introduction**

The Operational Plan is the cornerstone of the Stellarium Office, providing a detailed framework for establishing and managing a dynamic, collaborative environment within a mansion setting. It ensures that physical infrastructure, daily workflows, resource allocation, and contingency measures align with the Stellarium Foundation’s ethos of liberty, mutual benefit, and collective advancement.

**Mansion and Office Setup**

- **Mansion Specifications:** 6–11 bedrooms for 8–16 residents; 2,000 to 6,000 square feet; dedicated office space; reunion room with 75-inch 4K screen; fully equipped kitchen; dining room for 16–20; lounge; outdoor area.
- **Location Criteria:** Proximity to urban centers, yet situated in a serene, scenic area.
- **Technology and Equipment:** 4 laptops, 2 desktops, two 75-inch 4K TVs, reliable utilities with a generator.
- **Setup Timeline:** Week 1: Secure lease. Week 2: Purchase and install furniture, technology, and utilities.

**Resource Management**

- **Initial Investment:** Furniture: $10,000; Technology: $25,000; Miscellaneous: $10,000.
- **Ongoing Costs:** Monthly utilities: $800–$1600; Supplies: $700/month.
- **Inventory System:** Digital spreadsheet for monthly restocking.

**Staffing and Residents**

- **Staffing Process:** Recruitment via referrals and niche job boards, with a 2-day orientation.
- **Resident Selection:** Must be approved by John Victor, committed to Stellarium’s mission. Application includes an essay and interview.

**Scalability**

- **Initial Capacity:** Start with 6 residents and 2–6 staff, expanding to 16 residents.
- **Expansion Options:** Rent adjacent properties or transition to a larger facility.

**Daily Operations**

- **Work Schedule:** Morning (8:00 AM – 12:30 PM) for focused and collaborative tasks; Afternoon (12:30 PM – 5:30 PM) for communal lunch and project work; Evening (6:00 PM onward) for leisure and community events.
- **Task Management:** A physical Kanban board in the office and a shared collaborative digital board.
- **Visitor Engagement:** Visitors are welcome with prior approval for presentations, dinners, or parties.

**Contingency and Adaptation**

- **Budget Reserve:** Allocate $14,000 (12% of annual budget) for unexpected needs.
- **Adaptation Mechanisms:** Monthly operational reviews to tweak workflows based on feedback.

### **Community Living Agreement**

**Introduction**
