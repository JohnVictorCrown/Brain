# Order For Education

With the advances of AI and other technological advances, we need to revolutionize education and bring top education to all students up into undergraduate.

There are studies that demonstrate the advantages of AI in providing education enhancements capabilities. ([Study](https://news.harvard.edu/gazette/story/2024/09/professor-tailored-ai-tutor-to-physics-course-engagement-doubled/))

So I am sending an wide reaching order for the tech industry, people in education and others to start integrating AI into education to enhance education for students. In itself, AI models can serve and an indispensable private teacher that improves learning.

It is necessary that products for education are created, integrating with artificial intelligence, very simply an product with an app and website where the student can sign up, and and have access to all lectures according to their curriculum integrated with grading tests and AI tutoring, homework help and so on. A complete product for all things education. Integrating applications of learned knowledge onto real world applications and stimulating imaginative and engineering content.
