**Personal Finance**

The best way to increase your estate regardless of the level of wealth is to get better at managing your own money, it teaches you principles on how to honor your money and how to multiply effort. If you don't manage your personal finances wisely you probably don't know how to manage things wisely.

Here are the things you should know about personal finance. Sit down, get pen and paper and make an assessment of your finances: How do you make money? Is it a salary with other additional sources of income or you get paid for jobs you make such as actors, influencers, or are you making money as a business as salary and shareholder equity?

There are different approaches to know how to spend your money, here's what to pay attention to when managing your income: If your income varies a lot or is seasonal you need to set appropriate budgets that you allocate to yourself as a salary, let's say 2% of your Net Worth (Assets - Liabilities) or 30% of average income that you make a month, set a budget and spend your money accordingly.

Know what you spend your money on: There are different kinds of things you spend your money on: Things you actually need, these are basic bills, housing and food, basic utility bills; Things that make you more money such as businesses and investments; And Things you do for fun, leisure, pleasure or as hobbies.

Budget according to this: Only spend money on the things you really need, then **spend money on things that can make you more money, this is essential for estate building**, making more money needs to be a fun game and exciting to you, finding established and innovative ways to capitalize and potencialize your money on business ventures and other passion ventures needs to be fun and a go to on your budge. Then, only if you actually have fun, exciting and good experiences to invest, spend it, if you don't have any experience to spend on, allocate the budget to investing and building more estate.

Open your horizons and have an expansive mindset, a growth mindset, invest in expanding your business, your possibilities, your potential.

Unless you are billionaire, budget your personal finances, try to have your basic needs as little as possible according to your lifestyle, always opt for cost effective spending and keep your basic finances lean, that leaves more space for capitalizing discretionary income and having more fun experiences. Spend as little as you can in fixed bills according to your lifestyle, run through all your bills and don't spend money on any bills unless it's essential. If you need to decrease your spending on housing, consider making a mortgage and call your friends or some trusted people to come live with you so that you can charge them rent and help with the decreased spending in the mortgage, additionally you can team up with these people to be in relationships or business partnerships and just be great together while making money.

So here's what you should know about personal finances in sum: 

- Revise all of your basic bills and essential needs expenditure and spend as little as possible according to your budget.
- Spend all of the money you can in things that can make you more money, this is the most important thing in estate building, buying revenue making assets and business.
- Only spend money on fun, experiences or leisure if you have the need to and your budget allows for, otherwise don't spend any money or if you need more, make more money.
- Your Net Worth Is Your Assets - Liabilities, maximize that while investing heavily and money making enterprises.
